1. Who we are

Yea I Consulting is an AI strategy consultancy operated by Justin Yeaman, based in the United Kingdom. We help small and medium-sized businesses understand where AI can add genuine value to their operations and build a practical plan to get there.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, Justin Yeaman trading as Yea I Consulting is the data controller responsible for the personal information collected through this website and through our client engagements.

You can contact us at:

Yea I Consulting

Website: www.yea-i.com

2. What personal information we collect

We collect personal information in the following ways:

When you use the contact form or send us an email

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

When you complete the AI Readiness Diagnostic

  • Your name and email address
  • Your company name, role, and sector
  • Your responses to the diagnostic questions

The diagnostic is designed to help us provide you with a useful, personalised report. None of your responses are shared with third parties or used for any purpose other than generating that report and any subsequent work you engage us for.

When you book a call via Calendly

If you book a call using our Calendly scheduling link, Calendly will collect your name, email address, and any notes you provide. This is subject to Calendly's own privacy policy.

Website analytics

If we use website analytics tools, these may collect anonymised information about how visitors use our site — such as which pages are visited and how long visitors stay. We will update this policy if and when analytics are added, and will always use privacy-friendly options that do not require personal data collection where possible.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and messages
  • Generate and deliver your AI Readiness Diagnostic report
  • Provide the consulting services you've engaged us for
  • Send you information you've asked for, such as Insights articles
  • Fulfil any contractual obligations where a formal engagement exists
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We do not use your personal information for automated decision-making or profiling. We do not add you to any marketing lists without your explicit consent.

4. Our lawful basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following lawful bases for processing your personal data:

  • Legitimate interests — for responding to enquiries and providing information you've requested, where this does not override your rights and interests
  • Contract performance — where we have a formal engagement with you and need to process your data to fulfil it
  • Consent — for newsletter subscriptions and any optional communications you've explicitly opted into
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law

5. Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information with the following service providers who help us operate our business:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions and delivers them to our email inbox. Formspree is based in the United States and operates under standard contractual clauses for international data transfers. See Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Calendly — manages call scheduling. See Calendly's privacy policy.
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — the AI Readiness Diagnostic uses Claude to generate personalised briefing notes from your responses. Your diagnostic responses are sent to Anthropic's API for this purpose. Anthropic processes data in accordance with its privacy policy.
  • Netlify — hosts this website. Netlify may collect standard server logs including IP addresses. See Netlify's privacy policy.

We may also disclose your information where required to do so by law, or in connection with legal proceedings.

6. How long we keep your information

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Enquiry and contact form data — retained for up to 2 years from last contact, unless an ongoing engagement extends this
  • Diagnostic responses — retained for the duration of any subsequent engagement, plus 12 months thereafter
  • Client engagement records — retained for 6 years from the end of the engagement for accounting and legal purposes
  • Newsletter subscriber data — retained until you unsubscribe, and deleted within 30 days of an unsubscribe request

You can ask us to delete your information at any time — see Your rights below.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can ask us what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data (subject to any legal obligations we have to retain it)
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can ask us to provide your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Rights related to automated decision-making — we do not carry out automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects

To exercise any of these rights, email us at justin@yea-i.com. We'll respond within one month.

You also have the right to complain to the UK's Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you're not happy with how we've handled your data: ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

This website currently uses only essential functional cookies necessary to operate the site. We don't use advertising or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Our website is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are processed via Formspree's encrypted infrastructure.

No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take our obligations seriously and will notify you if a data breach occurs that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make significant changes, we'll make reasonable efforts to let you know — for example by email if we hold your contact details.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
